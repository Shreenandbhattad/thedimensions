About Us

Innovating spaces with cutting-edge design and technology

Founder Spotlight

Founded in 1996 by visionary director AR Sachin Bhattad, The Dimensions has been at the forefront of architectural innovation for nearly three decades. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for sustainable design, Sachin has led the firm to create spaces that inspire and endure. His leadership and commitment to excellence have earned The Dimensions a reputation as one of the leading architectural studios in Nashik.
